A flat little walk to an obvious door. You'll be dead in four seconds, and you won't see what did it. Then you'll do it again. And again.

PLAYif you dare 😈

The deal. Every trap is invisible until it's already killing you — a spike that stabs up from flat ground, a floor tile that drops away, a spike from the ceiling, a door that bolts when you reach for it.

You cannot dodge them the first time. That's the point. You die, you learn the spot, you try again. The whole gauntlet is the same every run — beat it by memory, not luck.
